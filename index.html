PADI courses and scuba diving in Rasdhoo

Rasdhoo, one of the Maldives' premier diving destinations, is the ideal location for scuba diving lovers. Dive with us and let us guide you to discover one of the most preserved environments of the Indian Ocean!

Big Blue Dive Rasdhoo is the perfect location for sea and scuba diving lovers on the island of Rasdhoo, Maldives. Discover beautiful and exciting scuba diving in the Indian Ocean, PADI diving courses for individuals, families, and groups, snorkeling trips for non-divers, equipment rental, and assistance with transfers, accommodation, and diving equipment. Ready to dive with us?

Diving in Rasdhoo

Thanks to its pristine location and protected marine area, Rasdhoo is known as one of the best diving destinations in the Maldives. Dive with us and discover the wonders of the Indian Ocean.

MORE INFO

PADI courses in Rasdhoo

Scuba diving allows you to discover the underwater world like no other activity. Big Blue Dive Rasdhoo offers a wide range of PADI diving courses for beginners or more advanced divers. Our experienced multilingual instructors and the crystal clear waters of Rasdhoo will make your scuba diving course an unforgettable experience.

MORE INFO

Snorkeling in Rasdhoo

The beauty of Rasdhoo's underwater world is already visible from the surface; all you need is fins/mask/snorkel to start exploring the fantastic waters full of life and colours! Big Blue Dive Rasdhoo offers you the opportunity to snorkel in Rasdhoo and explore several beautiful sites of the marine protected area.
